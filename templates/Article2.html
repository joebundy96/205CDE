Saint Preux suffers broken arm

Last night at UFC 197 Jon Jones went head to head with Ovince Saint Preux to determine the new interim UFC Light Heavyweight champion who will go on to face daniel cormier for the UFC Light Heavyweight championship. The fight saw jon jones earn himself a unanimous decision agaisnt saint Preux, however in the second round Jones went for a head kick which was blocked by Saint Preux breaking his arm in the process. Saint Preux went to fight another three rounds with a broken ar until the final bell rung. Saint Preux will need surgery on his arm which means he will be out of action possibly till next year. As for Jones, he wll be going on the face Daniel Cormier at UFC 200 for the new main event after Conor McGregr was pulled from the card ealier this week. UFC 200 will take place on 9th July 2016 in Las Vegas.
