Jones to go head to head with DC at UFC 200

After Jones defeated Saint Preux at UFC 197 this past saturday he made his opinions heard about his UFC 200 opponent and long time rival Daniel Cormier. There has been a lot of heat between these two competitors since their first bout for the UFC Light Heavyweight championship in January last year. The two fighters were constantly at each others throats leading up to their fisrt fight. During the staredown at a press confernece the two exchanged words before breaking out into a fight. The tension built and eventually they were able to release their anger on each other in the octogon. Jones picked u the victory rataining his title untill he later had to relinquish it after being suspended. This gave DC the opportunity to win the vacant UFC Light Heavyweight championship. Now both champions will go face to face in their long awaited rematch at UFC 200.
